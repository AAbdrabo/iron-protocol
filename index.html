1-MONTH PROGRESSION MAP
Your Transformation Phases
Phase-by-phase breakdown of what your body and lungs are doing. Check off each week when you complete it.
Week 1
Onboarding & Shock Phase
Muscle Lung

Expect heavy DOMS — your muscles haven't been trained this way. That soreness is signal, not damage. Focus entirely on learning what true mechanical failure feels like on The War set. Don't guess the weight — find your actual limit.

Lung: mucus begins thinning as you stack hydration with the herbal protocol. You may cough more in the morning. That's clearance — not regression.

Week 2
Neuromuscular Adaptation
CNS

Soreness drops significantly. Your central nervous system efficiency is climbing — the same weights feel lighter. This is not weakness leaving. This is your neural recruitment patterns getting cleaner and faster. Push harder now — your body is ready.

Lung: Ciliary function begins recovering. First signs of easier breathing during the Zone 2 treadmill work.

Week 3
Lung Clearing & Stamina
Lung

Airways feel noticeably more open. Recovery time between heavy sets decreases. You can push heavier on War sets because your cardiovascular system can now sustain the demand. The treadmill and tea protocol compounds here — this is where it pays off.

Load progression on The War sets should be clearly visible in your History log by now. Compare your Week 1 numbers.

Week 4
Muscle Density Solidification
Density

Myofibrillar hypertrophy kicks in. Muscles don't just look bigger — they feel harder and denser under the skin. Visual V-taper changes begin appearing in the mirror. Upper chest shelf and lat width become visible through clothing.

Lung: Four weeks of compound protocol. Exercise-induced breathlessness is measurably reduced versus your first session. This is your new baseline — now you build forward.
